Basic R guide for undergraduates in economics

This is a short guide to get students started on the basics of using the R language for statistical computing. It also provides links to further reading for those who wish to learn more.

ConnectFields game

This is a browser game I developed to help first-year students understand coordination bonuses in an engaging way. In the game, you manage one of two neighbouring farms participating in a payment-for-ecosystem services scheme (PES). Coordinate with the AI managing the adjacent farm to maximise the sum of farm outputs and PES payments.
